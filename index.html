Company Bio

PABRD is a team of experts from various fields of study including dairy production, animal nutrition, physiology, genetics, biotechnology, pasture agronomy, and food science and technology who aims to improve the local dairy animal's genes and produce better and cost-effective pastures in order to provide more productive dairy cattle and more quality milk for the Filipinos.
